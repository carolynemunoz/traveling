Budgeting and Saving

The currency is the Mexican Peso (MXN). Use local ATMs for the best exchange rates and avoid high exchange fees to save a little extra money.

Major expenses would be:

  • Flights, $200–$600 round trip from the U.S. or Canada (depending on season and airline). I would recommend traveling during late November or early May (great balance of weather and affordability).
  • Accommodation: $20–$200+ per night (hostels to luxury resorts).
  • Food: $5–$20 per meal (street tacos vs. upscale dining).
  • Transport: $1–$20 (buses, taxis, or car rentals).
  • Tours/Activities: $20–$100+ per experience (e.g., cenote swimming, ruins, snorkeling).

    • Traveling Tips

    Mexico has diverse regions, each offering unique experiences. Choose your destination based on your interests, travel style, and budget.

  • Beach Destinations: Cancun, Playa del Carmen, Tulum, Puerto Vallarta, Los Cabos.
  • Cultural and Historical Sites: Mexico City, Oaxaca, San Miguel de Allende, Puebla, Mérida.
  • Nature and Adventure: Chiapas, Copper Canyon, Baja California.

    • Some tips to make traveling a little easier are:

  • Language: Basic Spanish phrases are helpful, but English is widely spoken in tourist areas.
  • Safety: Stick to well-traveled areas and stay informed about local conditions. Use official taxis or ride-sharing apps.

    • Flying to Mexico

    Consider smaller regional airports in Mexico (e.g., Guadalajara or Mérida) for potentially lower costs compared to larger hubs like Cancun or Mexico City.

  • Some Budget Friendly Airlines: Volaris, Viva Aerobus, Frontier, Spirit Airlines (lower costs).
  • Full-Service Airlines: Aeroméxico, American Airlines, Delta, United.

    • Flight tips:

  • Book flights 2-3 months in advance for the best prices. Fly midweek, Tuesdays and Wednesdays for cheaper fares.
  • Tavel Insurance: protect yourself against unexpected events like cancellations or medical emergencies.
  • Packing: Lightweight clothing and comfortable shoes. Sunscreen, mosquito repellent, and a reusable water bottle. Copies of important documents (passport, ID).
