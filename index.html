Mexico

Geography and Nature

Mexico is located in southern North America, bordered by the United States to the north, the Pacific Ocean to the west, the Gulf of Mexico to the east, and Belize and Guatemala to the south.

It has diverse landscapes, including deserts, mountains, tropical rainforests, and beautiful coastlines.

The temperature usually ranges from arid in the north to tropical in the south. Coastal areas are humid, while higher altitudes like Mexico City have milder temperatures.

The Culture

Spanish is the official language, but 68 indigenous languages (e.g., Nahuatl, Maya) are also recognized.

They're predominantly Roman Catholic, with indigenous spiritual traditions often integrated.

Music and Dance: Mariachi, banda, and norteño are iconic musical styles. Traditional dances like the Jarabe Tapatío (Mexican Hat Dance) are widely performed.

Economy

Currency: Mexican Peso (MXN). The current conversion from dollars to pesos is 20.62.

In Mexico, credit/debit cards can be used but if you're going to shop at local markets and small towns then you should carry cash as they usually don't accept cards.

In high tourists places such as Cancun for example have adopted the use of dollars in whatever case you don't have pesos on you.
